Mapping My Year
by Nate Lambert


Welcome to the map of my year! This map is organized based off the chromatic scale, the most common musical scale in Western music. The scale contains twelve equally-spaced tones:



When any of the twelve tones is played against the resolving tone of the scale (in this case a "C"), one of twelve possible intervals is formed. Each interval has a distinct feeling attached to it, though the interpretation of these feelings may differ slightly depending on the listener. The twelve sites that I chose to portray in my map each correspond to one of the twelve musical intervals on the basis of the emotion that it evokes in me.
